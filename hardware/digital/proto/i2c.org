  - supports multiple devices on the same bus
  - must be assigned an unique address
  - Has no minimum CLOCK speed
  - Open drain output
  - you might need a =level shifter= to communicate from a 5v MCU to a 3v I2C device
  - pins
    - Vcc
    - GND
    - SCL(lock)
    - SDA(ta)
